Getting Started

Make Everything FASTsible

You don't need to go out to cash in through any physical outlets. Our agent comes to you.

First, enter your receiver's mobile number and pin their address on the map. Then, enter your address. Our map-matching technology locates nearby agents for both sender and receiver. Once an agent accepts, our agent comes to you and picks up your cash. After that, another agent delivers cash to your receiver. You can track both agents in real-time. Just sit back and enjoy.

About Us

Why Choose FastSend?

FastSend is a company founded in the Philippines to promote financial inclusion for all Filipino families.

FastSend aims to establish a nationwide network of FastSend agents using its userfriendly mobile app following Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas' directives to make financial services accessible to every Filipino. These services include sending and receiving money, online and offline transactions, paying bills, and purchasing necessities.
